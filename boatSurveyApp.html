MBTA Commuter Boat and Inner Harbor Ferry Survey - 2008-09

Shown on the page is a map displaying showing the 6 commuter boat docks serviced by surveyed routes. Users can select a dock in the combo box on the page, and then press Get Data to get five navigable data tables. The map will highlight the towns contributing different numbers of passengers from the selected dock, for example, 100 or more.
