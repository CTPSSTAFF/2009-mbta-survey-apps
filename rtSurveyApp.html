MBTA Rapid Transit Survey - 2008-09

Shown on the page is the MBTA rapid transit line spider diagram showing all lines and stations not including the Silver Line. Users can click on a station name on the diagram to generate a table of survey results. Alternatively the user can select the desired station in the combo box also on the page. Seven navigable data tables will be displayed.
